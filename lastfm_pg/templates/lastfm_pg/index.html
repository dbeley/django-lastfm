Last.fm tools

    On this website, you will find several tools to play with Last.fm data.

  • Scraping: Scraping tools.
    • Timeline: Export every scrobbles of a last.fm user.
    • Favorite: Export every favorite tracks of a last.fm user.
    • Genre: Export a list of artists of one or several genres (100 artists for each genre).
    • Artist infos: Extract infos about one or several artists.
  • Playlist Generator: Generate most played tracklist of a last.fm user.
  • Collage Generator: Last.fm collage generator.
  • Wordcloud Generator: Last.fm wordcloud generator.
