Lastfm tools

    On this website, you will find several tools to play with lastfm data.

    Heroku has a hard-limit of 30 seconds timeout on requests. Some long exports (timeline, user favorite tracks) might not work on heroku. Consider self-hosting.
  • Scraping: Scraping tools.
    • Timeline: Export every scrobbles of a lastfm user.
    • Favorite: Export every favorite tracks of a lastfm user.
    • Genre: Export a list of artists of one or several genres (100 artists for each genre).
    • Artist infos: Extract infos about one or several artists.
  • Playlist Generator: Generate most played tracklist of a lastfm user.
  • Collage Generator: Lastfm collage generator.
